About Us

Unlock Savings with swap2save - The Ultimate Coupon Exchange Platform

Swap2save is an online platform that helps people get rid of unwanted coupons from popular retailers like Amazon, Flipkart, and more. The platform also allows people to acquire such coupons from others who are willing to swap or sell them.

The idea for the platform came about when the founder, Vikram, was trying to get rid of some unwanted coupons he had. He realized that there was no easy way to do so and that there was a need for such a platform.

Since its launch in 2016, the platform has helped thousands of people save money on their shopping by getting rid of unwanted coupons.
